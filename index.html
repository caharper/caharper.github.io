    # About Me

    I am currently a PhD candidate in machine learning, where my focus is on exploring the intersection of of digital signal processing and neural networks. My aim is to explore ways to enhance the learning efficiency and capacity of neural networks, reducing reliance on extensive architecture design, boosting performance across various tasks, and saving computational and monetary resources. I love presenting complex concepts in a clear and engaging way, making technology more accessible to everyone. My passion lies in building AI products and systems that improve daily life by removing repetitive and mundane tasks, allowing people to focus on creativity and innovation. More
